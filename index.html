My name is Ben Shelley, I am a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho. Cooking and baking have always been one of my favorite things to do! I love cooking but especially eating what I cook. My favorite meal is breakfast, and my favorite breakfast food is crepes. But sadly with being a college student I don't really ever have the time to cook something that complicated and time consuming. And I really am not a Fan of frozen foods, which is why as a student i have spent some time figuring out what food I can make that still tastes good but most importantly don't contain a crazy amount of ingredients, is least expensive, and doesn't take a long time to cook because I have a busy schedule.
