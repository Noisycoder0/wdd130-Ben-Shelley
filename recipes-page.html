Food & Recipes

These meals are designed to be affordable, filling, and easy to prepare with minimal ingredients. They’re broken down by meal type so you can quickly find something that fits your schedule.

Dinner

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is an easy, classic meal that’s filling and inexpensive. Start by boiling water in a medium pot on high heat. Once boiling, add noodles (optional: 1 tablespoon of salt). Cook according to package directions. Brown 1 lb of ground beef in a pan until fully cooked (160°F), drain excess fat, and mix with spaghetti sauce. Combine with noodles, serve, and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Seasoning of choice

Burritos

Cook rice using a rice cooker or pot. Brown ground turkey in a pan until fully cooked (165°F) with seasonings. Add rice, meat, salsa, and toppings to a tortilla and enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Burrito tortillas
  • Rice (1 cup rice : 2 cups water)
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • Salsa
  • Optional toppings

Spicy Cheesy Ramen

Boil ramen noodles for 4 minutes and 30 seconds, then drain. In a separate pan, melt butter and add milk and cheese until a thick sauce forms. Mix with noodles and serve.

Ingredients:

  • Nongshim Shin Ramyun
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1½ cups milk
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Lunch

Tacos

Cook ground beef until fully cooked (165°F). Heat tortillas and assemble with meat, salsa, and toppings of choice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Small corn tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Optional toppings

Chicken and Rice

Cook rice using a rice cooker. Cut chicken into cubes, season, and cook in a pan until no longer pink and reaches 165°F. Serve together.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Rice (1 cup rice : 2 cups water)
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Olive oil

Grilled Cheese

Butter bread, add cheese and optional lunch meat, and grill in a pan until golden brown and melted. Serve with tomato soup if desired.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Sliced cheese
  • Optional lunch meat

Breakfast

Pancakes / Waffles

Mix pancake batter with water (or optional ingredients). Cook pancakes on a pan or waffles in a waffle iron until golden brown.

Ingredients:

  • Pancake mix
  • Water
  • Optional: egg, oil

Breakfast Sandwich

Cook bacon or sausage, fry eggs, stack with cheese on bread or an English muffin, and serve.

Ingredients:

  • Bread or English muffin
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Bacon or sausage
